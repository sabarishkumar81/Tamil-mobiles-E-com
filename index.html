Customer Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your active orders by navigating to the "My Orders" tab on the bottom menu or inside your Account section. Once the order status is updated by the store administrator, you will receive real-time updates.

What are the payment options?

We currently support Cash on Delivery (COD) and direct order routing through WhatsApp. You can coordinate your preferred online payment method (UPI, GPay, PhonePe, or Bank Transfer) directly with our support team after initiating a checkout.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your order by clicking the "Cancel Order" button in your Order History page before the order is dispatched, or by contacting our team directly on WhatsApp at +91 81244 76063.

Shipping & Delivery Policy

At Tamil Mobiles, we strive to deliver your products as quickly and safely as possible. Please read our shipping guidelines below:

  • Delivery Timelines: Standard delivery takes 1 to 3 business days within Tamil Nadu, and 3 to 6 business days for other states across India.
  • Shipping Costs: We charge a flat delivery rate of ₹50 on all orders. Free shipping is automatically applied on orders above ₹999.
  • Dispatch Time: All orders placed before 4:00 PM are dispatched on the same business day. Orders placed after this cut-off will be processed on the next business day.
  • Order Verification: A support executive may contact you via phone or WhatsApp to verify your shipping details before dispatching your package.

Returns & Refunds Policy

We offer a customer-friendly returns and replacements window to ensure you are fully satisfied with your purchase:

  • 7-Day Window: We offer a 7-day hassle-free replacement policy for defective, damaged, or incorrect products from the date of delivery.
  • How to Return: Simply navigate to your order screen and click "Request Return", or message our team on WhatsApp with your Order ID and photos of the issue.
  • Refund Method: Once the returned item is verified, refunds are processed instantly via Google Pay, PhonePe, or UPI Transfer.
  • Exceptions: Customized items, tampered glass screen guards that have been peeled/applied, and items missing their original packaging/barcodes are not eligible for returns.

We respect your privacy and protect your personal information with secure storage systems:
